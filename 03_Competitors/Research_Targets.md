## Merchandising Benchmark

### VAT19

Study only for:

- Product photography

- Gift presentation

- Product storytelling

- Bundling

- Product-page layout

- Review placement

- Upsells

Do not include VAT19 in soda pricing averages unless the product is directly comparable.

## Required Pricing Fields

For every comparable product:

- Competitor

- Product name

- Product type

- Bottle count

- Total price

- Price per bottle

- Shipping price

- Whether shipping is included

- Subscription or one-time purchase

- Subscription frequency

- Source URL

- Capture date

- Notes

## Required Subscription Fields

- Box type

- Bottle count

- Monthly price

- Delivered price if shipping is known

- Recurring or prepaid

- 3-month option

- 6-month option

- 12-month option

- Cancellation language

- Skip or pause option

- Gift option

- Product selection method

- Duplicate policy
