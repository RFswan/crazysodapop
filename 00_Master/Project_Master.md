**PROJECT OVERVIEW**

Crazy Soda Pop is a premium soda discovery company built around nostalgia, discovery, gifting, and unique beverage experiences.

Crazy Soda Pop is an online specialty soda retailer focused on unique glass bottled sodas, curated collections, and premium gifting. The Crazy Soda Pop Club is the flagship recurring subscription experience, but customers can also purchase individual bottles, variety packs, themed collections, gifts, and limited-edition products.

The goal is not to sell beverages.

The goal is to create excitement, anticipation, nostalgia, and memorable experiences through soda.

---
