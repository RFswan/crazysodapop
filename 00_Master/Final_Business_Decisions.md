\## BRAND ARCHITECTURE



Parent company and website:



\*\*Crazy Soda Pop\*\*



Flagship subscription program:



\*\*The Soda Discovery Club\*\*



The Soda Discovery Club is not the name of the entire website.



Crazy Soda Pop will support:



\- Individual soda bottles

\- 6-packs

\- 12-packs

\- Curated packs

\- Build-a-Pack

\- Candy

\- Gifts

\- The Soda Discovery Club

\- Future seasonal and international collections



\---



\## PRIMARY AUDIENCE



Primary subscription audience:



\- Adults age 35 and older

\- Nostalgia buyers

\- Soda collectors

\- Gift shoppers

\- Families looking for shared experiences

\- Customers interested in unusual and hard-to-find sodas



The website must feel adult, polished, trustworthy, and fun.



It must not feel childish or like a novelty party store.



\---

\## SHIPPING



\- Free shipping on merchandise orders of $39 or more within the continental United States.

\- Orders under $39 use calculated carrier shipping rates.

\- Every Soda Discovery Club subscription includes free shipping within the continental United States.



Do not claim free shipping outside the continental United States.



\---



\## HOMEPAGE SOCIAL PROOF



Approved social-proof statements:



\- 100,000+ Orders Shipped

\- Free Shipping Over $39

\- Free Shipping on Every Subscription



Existing reviews from Amazon or other marketplaces may appear only in a section clearly labeled:



\*\*What Customers Say About Crazy Soda Pop\*\*



Marketplace reviews must not be presented as reviews of The Soda Discovery Club unless they came from actual club subscribers.



Do not fabricate customer reviews, names, ratings, statistics, or testimonials.

\## THE SODA DISCOVERY CLUB



Two subscription box sizes will be offered:



\### 6 Bottle Club



\- Monthly: $29.99

\- 3-month option: $28.99 per shipment

\- 6-month option: $27.99 per shipment

\- 12-month prepaid option: $299 total



\### 12 Bottle Club



\- Monthly: $49.99

\- 3-month option: $47.99 per shipment

\- 6-month option: $45.99 per shipment

\- 12-month prepaid option: $499 total



Shipping is included for every subscription within the continental United States.



The exact billing and fulfillment setup must be tested inside Shopify before launch.

\## ONE-TIME PURCHASES

\- Individual bottles

Subscription messaging must be prominent, but it must not make the website appear subscription-only.

\## PACKAGING



Current packaging is functional and honest:



\- Plain corrugated shipping box

\- Every glass bottle individually bubble wrapped

\- Protective packing material as needed



Do not depict branded subscription boxes that do not exist.



Approved homepage imagery may show:



\- A plain kraft shipping box

\- Real glass soda bottles

\- Root beers and specialty sodas

\- Realistic protective packing



Do not create fake branded packaging.

\## CANDY



Candy is not automatically included in every subscription.



Candy may be offered as:



\- A one-time add-on

\- A recurring subscription add-on

\- An occasional surprise

\- A gift upgrade



Pricing must be based on actual product, labor, packaging, and shipping costs before launch.



\---



\## LOYALTY AND BONUSES



Do not promise expensive fixed milestone gifts before wholesale costs and subscriber volume are known.



Possible future bonuses include:



\- Candy

\- Bonus soda

\- Bottle opener

\- Soda-themed socks

\- Apparel

\- Other inexpensive surprise items



Website language should preserve flexibility.



Approved general language:



\*\*Members may receive occasional bonus treats and surprises throughout the year.\*\*



Do not promise lifetime pricing.



\---



\## VISUAL DIRECTION



Approved visual direction:



\- Warm cream

\- Vintage red

\- Muted teal

\- Cola brown or dark charcoal

\- Authentic glass-bottle soda photography

\- Premium nostalgia

\- Clear ecommerce hierarchy

\- Adult and polished presentation



Avoid:



\- Neon colors

\- Cartoon mascots

\- Comic fonts

\- Cheap gradients

\- Fake vintage history

\- Generic AI-looking product labels

\- Fake branded boxes

\- Overly cluttered layouts

\- Excessive animation



\---



\## PROHIBITED LANGUAGE



Never use:



\- Est. 2026

\- Established 2026

\- Since 2026

\- Fake historical claims

\- Fake subscription reviews

\- Unsupported sales claims

\- Unsupported scarcity claims

\## WEBSITE GOAL



Within five seconds, visitors should understand:



1\. Crazy Soda Pop sells specialty sodas.

2\. Customers can shop without subscribing.

3\. The Soda Discovery Club delivers curated soda boxes.

4\. Both 6-bottle and 12-bottle subscriptions are available.

5\. Candy, packs, and gifts are also available.



\---



\## ECONOMICS



Current estimated margin on a $49.99 12-bottle shipment is approximately $12 after product and shipping costs.



Customer lifetime value is unknown because the subscription program is new.



Annual plans are intended to:



\- Improve retention

\- Generate upfront cash flow

\- Help fund inventory and fulfillment



Before launch, pricing should also be tested against:



\- Payment-processing fees

\- Subscription-app fees

\- Damage and replacement costs

\- Packaging costs

\- Labor

\- Promotional bonuses

\- Shipping-zone differences
