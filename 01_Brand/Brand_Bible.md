## Brand story and positioning (approved brand guide + Final_Business_Decisions)

**Story theme:** Soda as adventure—Route 66, corner stores, and the era when soda felt special; curating hard-to-find, small-batch, and classic favorites.

**Positioning:** Premium destination for unique **glass bottled** sodas.

**Crazy Soda Pop is not:**

- A candy store
- A mass retailer
- A discount shop

**Crazy Soda Pop is:**

- A curator
- A storyteller
- A premium experience

(Aligned with Final_Business_Decisions: not childish, not a novelty party store; adult, polished, trustworthy, fun.)

---

## Target customer (approved brand guide + Final_Business_Decisions)

- **Age:** 35–65+
- **Income:** Middle to upper middle
- **Interests:** Nostalgia, road trips, Americana, collecting, unique foods and drinks, family experiences, gift giving
- **Values:** Quality, discovery, experiences over things, supporting small businesses

**Motivations:** Discovery, nostalgia, entertainment, family experiences, gifts, surprise, memories, connection.

## Voice and tone (approved brand guide)

| Attribute | Guidance |
|-----------|----------|
| **Tone** | Friendly, warm, nostalgic, conversational |
| **Style** | Clear, authentic, story-driven; educate and inspire without talking down |

**We say (examples from approved brand guide):**

- Every bottle has a story
- Hand-picked. Not mass picked.
- Made to be shared.
- Discover something amazing.

**We don't say:**

- Limited time only!
- Act now!
- Supplies are running out!
- You need this!

**Prohibited (Final_Business_Decisions):** Est. / Established / Since 2026; fake historical claims; fake subscription reviews; unsupported sales or scarcity claims; **star ratings until verified from an approved source**.

## Imagery and packaging (approved brand guide + Final_Business_Decisions)

**Photography:**

- Warm, authentic, nostalgic settings with real products
- Textures, labels, glass bottles, vintage details
- People sharing sodas where appropriate
- Route 66 and Americana details as supporting art

**Icons:** Simple, vintage-inspired, clean lines, handcrafted feel.

**Packaging truth (Final_Business_Decisions):**

- Plain corrugated shipping box
- Each glass bottle individually bubble wrapped
- Protective packing as needed
- Do **not** depict branded subscription boxes that do not exist
- Approved imagery: plain kraft box, real bottles, realistic packing

Do **not** add “Ships from North Carolina” or similar origin claims until explicitly approved.

**Design accents (approved brand guide):** Route US 66 shield, CSP seal, sketch-style bottles and crates as secondary background elements—use sparingly; avoid cluttered layouts.

## Product scope (Final_Business_Decisions)

Crazy Soda Pop supports: individual bottles, 6-packs, 12-packs, curated packs, Build-a-Pack, candy, gifts, The Soda Discovery Club, and future seasonal/international collections.

Candy is not automatically included in every subscription.

Gift products may exist in catalog; **Gift Boxes is not a required primary homepage section for v1** (see `05_Website/Homepage_Blueprint.md`).

---

## Loyalty language (Final_Business_Decisions)

Approved general language: **Members may receive occasional bonus treats and surprises throughout the year.**

Do not promise lifetime pricing or expensive fixed milestone gifts before costs and volume are known.
