## Approved v1 section order (top to bottom)

1. **Announcement bar**  
   Shipping and trust messaging per Final_Business_Decisions (not mockup “all orders free shipping”).

2. **Header and navigation**  
   Frozen v1 nav: Shop Soda (Single Bottles, 6 Packs, 12 Packs), Soda Discovery Club, Build a Pack, Candy, About Us. Provisional logo via editable image setting.

3. **Hero**  
   Headline: *New Soda. Every Month. Delivered To Your Door.*  
   Supporting line: *Remember when soda was exciting?*  
   Primary CTA: *Start Your First Box*. Secondary: *Shop Soda*.

4. **Social-proof strip**  
   Approved non-review statements only (e.g. 100,000+ Orders Shipped, Free Shipping Over $39, Free Shipping on Every Subscription—continental U.S. per Final). **No star ratings.**

5. **Shop Soda / category tiles**  
   Entry to catalog and pack types (singles, 6-packs, 12-packs). Visual direction from mockup “SHOP ALL” column where applicable.

6. **Build a Pack**  
   Dedicated block linking to Build a Pack experience (mockup column reference).

7. **Soda Discovery Club**  
   Subscription value prop; 6- and 12-bottle paths (mockup “SUBSCRIPTIONS” column and hero CTAs where applicable).

8. **How It Works**  
   Three-step club flow (mockup “HOW THE CLUB WORKS” section).

9. **Candy**  
   Candy catalog entry (mockup “CANDY” column where applicable).

10. **Packaging and shipping trust**  
    Honest packaging: plain corrugated box, bubble-wrapped bottles, $39+ free shipping and subscription shipping rules—**no** “Ships from North Carolina” until approved.

11. **What Customers Say About Crazy Soda Pop**  
    Marketplace reviews only, clearly labeled. **Content empty until approved marketplace reviews are supplied.** No fabricated testimonials or ratings.

12. **FAQ**  
    Common questions; layout TBD beyond standard accordion/list pattern.

13. **Email signup**  
    List growth; copy and incentive TBD in admin/theme settings (do not invent offers).

14. **Footer**  
    Supporting links: FAQ, Contact, Shipping, Policies (per Final_Business_Decisions).
